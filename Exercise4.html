ICT

Information and communication technology (ICT) in education is the approach to education that makes use of ICT to support, improve, and optimize information delivery. International studies have demonstrated that ICT can increase student learning and teaching techniques.




Computer Science

Computer scientists are responsible for the design, development, and analysis of the hardware and software that are used to address issues in a variety of professional, industrial, academic, and social contexts. There is a substantial human component to computer science as well because computers solve issues to benefit and enhance people.
